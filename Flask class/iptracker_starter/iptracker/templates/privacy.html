Privacy Notice

This educational demo logs your public IP, user-agent, page path, and a timestamp to a private Firebase Realtime Database.

  • Purpose: classroom demonstration of logging/analytics.
  • Retention: records older than 30 days may be pruned.
  • Control: if logged in, you can delete records associated with your session user from the Dashboard.
  • Security: server-side writes with Firebase Admin SDK; database rules are locked down.

For production, replace the teaching login with Firebase Authentication and consider anonymizing IPs.
